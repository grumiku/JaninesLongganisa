GOT ANY QUESTIONS?

1. What is Longganissa?

A: Longganissa is a Filipino-style sausage made from ground pork, garlic, vinegar, sugar, and various spices. It is often eaten for breakfast with eggs and rice, or as a filling for sandwiches.

2. What is a Longganissa shop?

A: A Longganissa shop is a store that specializes in selling Longganissa sausages, typically made fresh daily.

3. Where can I find Longganissa shops?

A: Longganissa shops can be found in many Filipino communities and neighborhoods, as well as in specialty food markets and online stores.

4. What types of Longganissa are available at a Longganissa shop?

A: Longganissa shops typically offer a variety of flavors, including sweet, garlicky, spicy, and tangy. Some may also offer different types of Longganissa, such as skinless or skin-on, and different sizes.

5. How long does Longganissa last?

A: Fresh Longganissa should be consumed within a few days of purchase, or frozen for later use. Cooked Longganissa can be stored in the refrigerator for up to a week or frozen for up to 3 months.

6. Are Longganissa sausages healthy?

A:Longganissa is a high-fat, high-sodium food and should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet. It is not recommended for individuals with heart disease or high blood pressure.

7. Can I order Longganissa online?

A: Yes, many Longganissa shops offer online ordering and shipping for customers outside of their local area.
