ABOUT THE LONGGANISA SHOP

The Urbesa longganisa is very famous and well known around Divisoria. They started on August 10, 2010, 12 years ago, and they cater delicious processed foods like longganisa, skinless, and embutido. Longganisa is the best product among the other processed foods that Urbesa longganisa is selling. Because this longganisa was originally made by the Urbesa family, they inherited the recipe from their ancestors. All the processes and ingredients are locally made, and over the generations, they passed on the legacy of their business to the next generation of the Family. The urbesa longganisa are now handled and managed by Miss Janine Urbesa Manager and owner of Urbesa Longganisa. Janine is now handling the business, and according to her, they are continuously establishing the quality of the product, providing the best, and creating new flavors of longganisa, but the original recipe of their longganisa is still in their variety. Urbesa Longganisa is also affordable, and you can choose other varieties of their products in a cheap and very affordable way, but surely you won't regret buying some of their products. They are located near the night market at Divisoria, making it very accessible to visit the store. You can also visit their website if you want to order online. Urbesa longganisa is also open for reselling if you want to start your own business. So if you want to try and experience the delicious longganisa, come and see their original home-made longganisa, Urbesa Longganisa.
