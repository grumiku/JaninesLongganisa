ABOUT THE LONGGANISA SHOP

The Urbesa longganisa are very famous and well known around Divisoria. They started on August 2010 12yrs from now and thy cater delicious processed food like longganisa, skinless and embutido, and longganisa are the best product among other processed food that Urbesa longganisa are selling. Because this longganisa are made originally by the Urbesa Family, they inherited the recipe from their ancestors. All the process and ingredients are locally made and by the generations they passed to their family the legacy of their bussiness in the next generation of Urbesa clan. The urbesa longganisa are now handling and managing by Miss Janine Urbesa Manager and owner of Urbesa Longganisa. Janine is now handling the bussiness and accordint to her, they continuosly establishing the good quality of the product, providing the best and creating new flavors of longganisa, but the original recipe of their longganisa are still in their variety. Urbesa Longganisa, are also affordable and you can choose other variety of their products in a cheap and very affordable but surely you won't regret to buy some of their products. They located to the night market at Divisoria very accesible to visit the store, you can also visit their website if you want to order online. Urbesa longganisa are also open for reselling if you want to start your own business. So if you want to try and experiences the delicious longganisa, come and see their original home made longganisa of Urbesa Longganisa.
